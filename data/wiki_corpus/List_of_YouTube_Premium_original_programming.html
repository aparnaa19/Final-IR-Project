From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

YouTube Premium (formerly Music Key and YouTube Red) is a subscription service that provides advertising-free streaming of all videos hosted by YouTube, offline play and background playback of videos on mobile devices, access to advertising-free music streaming through YouTube Music, and access to "YouTube Original" series and films. In January 2022, YouTube announced it was shutting down its "YouTube Originals" program.[1] Following is a list of all past "YouTube Original" scripted series, unscripted series, feature-length films and documentaries.

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Google's Street View program in Argentina began with the filming of streets and roads in October 2013. On September 25, 2014, most of the country's cities were made available online, including Buenos Aires, Córdoba, Rosario and Mendoza. As of September 2015[update], coverage is high, with over three-fourths of cities with over 25,000 people having most of its streets photographed.
